Our Mission

To foster connection, creativity, and care for all who call Echo Park home.

Our Story

Founded in 1997 by local residents, Echo Point Community Center was born from a vision of shared space where people of all backgrounds could access resources, build friendships, and thrive together. Today, we continue that work with inclusive programming, creative workshops, and a welcoming home for all ages.
